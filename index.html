What's the difference from V2 and V1 mini's?‚ï≤‚ï±

The V1 mini drifter is a 1:76 scale, while the V2 is a 1:43 scale. The V2 is slightly bigger but is not made with metal and is more affordable. The V2 includes practice cones and also rubber tires which the V1 does not. Overall, it is a more affordable way to get into RC drifting with some extra accessories.
